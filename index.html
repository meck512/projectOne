Music, Memories, Friends. Why Wait?

Whoever told you impulse decisions are bad, are probably sitting on their couch right now reminicing the good old days while watching the same news cast from 2 hours ago. Search your favorite artist & get a list of their next 5 shows & go create some memories with your closest friends. Need a hotel? We have you covered. Need a flight? Well you're on your own but hey? We still did most of the leg work for you. Go have a great time, take some pictures, and one day when that re-run news cast comes on you'll have ridiculous memories to tell the grandkids about instead.

Check out one of these popular artists, or search one of your favorites!

About Us

With the Pandemic, more and more people like us are excited to get out and about and travel to see live music. We're two people learning web development that really enjoyed creating this application. Thanks for checking it out, and go have an experience of a lifetime!
