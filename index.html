About Us

With the Pandemic, more and more people are excited to get out and about and travel to see live music. If you love music and traveling, Impulse Concerts helps satisfy both of those needs. In a quick search, Impulse Concerts will load your last 5 artist searches, find their upcoming venues, and give you hotel recommendations to scratch that music and travel itch.
